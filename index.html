About Me

QA is a System,
Not Just Testing

Hi, I'm Ritesh Solanki — a Quality Engineering Leader with 15+ years of experience building and scaling QA practices across Mobile, Web, and API platforms.

I've led quality initiatives for Truecaller (50M+ monthly active users) and Sixer by Dream Sports Group, consistently maintaining less than 1% production defect leakage — even at a pace of weekly releases.

My approach to QA goes beyond finding bugs. I focus on building systems — from establishing end-to-end QA processes and defect tracking pipelines, to mentoring high-performing teams and aligning quality goals with business outcomes.

I've built QA teams from the ground up (15+ engineers), reduced defect leakage by 35%, and improved release cycles by 20%. I'm deeply passionate about the intersection of AI and Quality Engineering — leveraging GenAI tools to accelerate test case generation, automate documentation, and improve regression planning — boosting test design productivity by nearly 40%.

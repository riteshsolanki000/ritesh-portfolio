My Approach

How I build QA from scratch.

A proven 4-step framework for turning QA chaos into confident releases.

01

Audit & Align

Understand the product, team, and risk areas. Align QA goals with business outcomes before writing a single test case.

02

Build Foundation

Set up JIRA, Notion, Postman. Define defect taxonomy. Create the Test Strategy and first Test Plans that the team can actually use.

03

Embed in Delivery

Integrate QA into sprint ceremonies — planning, triage, release governance. Make quality a team sport, not a bottleneck.

04

Scale & Optimise

Hire and mentor QA engineers, introduce automation, and iterate using Datadog and production data to continuously raise the bar.
